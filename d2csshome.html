Washing Machine

A washing machine is an automated appliance that cleans laundry by using water, detergent, and mechanical action to remove dirt from clothes. It works by agitating or tumbling clothes in a drum filled with water and soap, followed by rinsing and spinning to remove excess water. Key components include an inner drum (or tub) to hold clothes, an outer tub to contain water, a motor to spin the drum, a pump to drain water, and a control panel to manage cycles.A washing machine is a home appliance that automates the process of cleaning laundry by using a combination of water, detergent, and mechanical action to loosen and remove dirt from clothes.
